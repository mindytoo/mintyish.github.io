The Engineer

Hello there! I'm Mindy, a senior pursuing a Mechanical Engineering degree at Olin College of Engineering. After college I hope to bring my degree with me into the field of assistive technology to build prosthetics that blend seamlessly into our lives. Until then, I'm making the most of my adventures here at Olin with the courses, research, and just fun life opportunities with my teammates and peers. For more of my past experience and publications, please see my resume.

Outside of academics, I craft, run, and play videogames. My current side projects include props for cosplay at this year's upcoming PAX East convention here in Boston!
