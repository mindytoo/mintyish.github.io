The Engineer

Hello there! I'm Mindy, a third year pursuing a Mechanical Engineering degree at Franklin W. Olin College. After college I hope to bring my degree with me into the field of assistive technology to build prosthetics that blend seamlessly into our lives. Until then, I'm making the most of my adventures here at Olin with the courses, research, and just fun life opportunities.

This summer I will be researching under Chris Lee and supported by the MA Space Grant. I'll be working with a dentist on a pacifier designed to avoid overbite flare in children and infants and with fellow students on developing bio-mimetic perching landing gear for unmanned aerial drones.

Also planned is a fall 2015 semester in Tokyo, Japan where I'll be studying arts and sciences at Sophia University!

The Person

Originating from Montgomery, NJ, I graduated high school with curiosity for the STEM field and flamboyant personality to help me explore it. Outside of the "Olin bubble", you can probably find me perusing Boston in my constant quest for food. Trying to escape tedium, I enjoy playing the tourist and going to see all the new MFA exhibits and Boston street events. Or I will be in the side streets of North End consuming gelatto and wading through book stores. My weekday default escape however, is usually ordering Thai with friends and watching anime, playing video games, or going on runs along the Wellesley trails.
