The Engineer

Hello there! I'm Mindy, a third year pursuing a Mechanical Engineering degree at Franklin W. Olin College. After college I hope to bring my degree with me into the field of assistive technology to build prosthetics that blend seamlessly into our lives. Until then, I'm making the most of my adventures here at Olin with the courses, research, and just fun life opportunities.

My Latest Project

This past summer I researched under Chris Lee and was supported by the Massachusetts Space Grant to design, fabricate, and test a new perching landing gear for unmanned aerial vehicles. In parallel I worked with Dr. David Tesini, conducting a finite element analysis on his two new orthodontic pacifier designs and comparing their deformations and stresses on the upper palate to commercially available models.
