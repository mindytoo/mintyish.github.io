Fluffiness at the Akiba Owl Cafe.

9 . 22 . 15

Alex & Mindy & friends fluffle owls in Akihabara.


Akiba フクロウ

Alex:Kevin and I met up with Mindy and another friend of ours, Chiaki, to go visit the owl cafe in Akihabara! The cafe wasn’t really a cafe but more of a petting zoo. They gave us some waters and let us walk around the small room to pet most of the owls. If we wanted to, we could ask the caretakers to put the owls on our arms while we held a small leash attached to their foot. We all carried a few different ones but my favorite was a large, angry looking one named Negi and Mindy liked a smaller, chubby one who liked to sit on people’s shoulders named Okra. We had an hour total there and after we were done we headed to Hanamaru to get some udon for dinner. Mindy, Kevin, and I went to a small bar/restaurant to sit around a talk before splitting up for a day.
