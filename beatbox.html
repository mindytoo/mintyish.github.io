Analog Beatbox Designing a Circuit Base and Snare

The Project

After a semester of Modeling and Controls, a partner and I joined our new found circuit skills and our interest for DJ-ing into an original analog beat box. The final presentation can be found on this instructable (which had over 2k views in two weeks!). Although the concept is simple, it could easily be scaled up to include dozens of different sounds. At the core of our project was a white noise generator that would create all the frequencies we were interested in using for our beat box. Then we created two series of filters in parallel to get a bass and snare sound which were then activated and deactivated by a square wave generated by op amps. The frequencies of each could be tuned by separate potentiometers.
