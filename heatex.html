Heat Exchanger A Sojourn into the Thermodynamics of "Beer" and "Coffee"

The Project

Ever had uncomfortably hot beer and undrinkably cold coffee? Inspired by a guest lecture by Professor Brian Storey, fellow student, Hannah Wilk and I recreated his "magical coffee and beer heat exchanging machine" using a water bath and some common household materials. What ensued was a wet and slow recreation of a heat exchanger. Though our method would only cool both fluids to the same temperature, we had a fun sojourn into the thermodynamics of heat exchange.
